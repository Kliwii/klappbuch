  • Welcome

    The mixmatch app is a participatory application that presents the diversity of people in a creative and playful way. The app is inspired by the mix & match books you probaly know from your childhood. The books consisted of 3 parts that could be combined in any way and allowed the creation of funny combinations. Let's see how it works.

  • Swipe

    Discover fun matches! You can see new slices by swiping left and right.

  • Tap

    To switch the camera view to a bodypart of your choice just tap on it.

  • Record

    Press record to become a part of the application yourself. Be creative.

  • Edit

    Tap on options to play (or interact) with the videos. Change the playback position, turn off/on the sound and upload your recorded video.
