  • Welcome

    Do you remember those mix & match books from your childhood? We created a digital version.

    With mixmatch you can create fun combinations of body parts and add your own to the mix. If you want you can upload your video to appear here and in an installation at Furtwangen University. The goal is to display diversity of people all around the world.

    Letâ€™s see how it works!

  • Swipe

    Swipe left and right for new slices and discover fun matches!

  • Tap

    Tap on the slice where you want to activate your camera!

  • Record

    Press record to capture your video on your device. After the countdown, it's exactly 4 seconds of showtime! Be creative!

    If you want to become a part of the mixmatch installation yourself, you can upload your video afterwards.

  • Edit

    Use the "Edit" button to enter edit mode. You can now change the playback position of each video loop, so all the slices fit just right.
